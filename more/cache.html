Item Shop

What is the Item Shop?

The DuckMath Item Shop (nicknamed "Cache") is where you can spend coins earned through playing games to purchase exclusive cosmetic items, custom cursors, backgrounds, badges, and seasonal rewards. Transform your gaming experience with personalized content that makes DuckMath truly yours!

How to Earn Coins

  • 🎮 Playing Games: Earn coins for every game session and high scores you achieve
  • 📅 Daily Logins: Get bonus coins for maintaining your daily streak
  • 🎯 Completing Challenges: Special events and daily challenges reward extra coins
  • 🏆 Leaderboard Rankings: Top players earn bonus coins and exclusive rewards
