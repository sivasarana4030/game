What is Tab Cloak?

Tab Cloak is a privacy feature that disguises the appearance of your browser tabs, making it less obvious what content you're viewing. When activated, your gaming tabs will appear as innocent websites like "Google Classroom" or "Khan Academy" to maintain your privacy.
