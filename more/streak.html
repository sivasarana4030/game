Streak

What is Streak?

Streak is a gamification feature that tracks consecutive days of gaming activity on DuckMath. It encourages consistent engagement by rewarding players who return regularly to play games, creating a sense of progression and achievement.

How Does Streak Work?

  • 🔥 Daily Tracking: Automatically records when you play games each day
  • 🔥 Consecutive Counting: Builds your streak for each day you return
  • 🔥 Milestone Rewards: Unlock achievements and rewards at specific streak lengths
  • 🔥 Progress Visualization: See your streak history and current progress

Streak Rewards & Milestones

🥉 Week Warrior (7 days)

Special badge & profile customization

🥈 Month Master (30 days)

Exclusive games access & themes

🥇 Legendary (100 days)

VIP Discord role & early features

Building Your Streak

Your streak increases each day you visit DuckMath and play at least one game. The timer resets at midnight (your local time), giving you a full 24-hour window to maintain your streak. If you miss a day, your streak resets to zero, but you can start building again immediately!

💡 Pro Tips for Maintaining Streaks

  • Set a daily reminder to visit DuckMath
  • Play a quick game during lunch or breaks
  • Check your streak progress on the leaderboard
  • Join Discord for streak accountability partners!
